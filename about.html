About GamZ

India's first student-built indie PC gaming store. We build games, you play them.

Our Story

GamZ was born in a college dorm during the 6th semester of a B.Tech Computer Science program. Four friends — passionate about gaming and technology — decided to stop just playing games and start building them. What started as a semester project became something much bigger.

We build every game from scratch. Every line of code, every pixel — ours. No copyright issues, no licensing fees. Just pure indie gaming, made in India, for the world.

🇮🇳 Made in India — Every GamZ title is an original, student-created indie game. Your purchase directly supports young Indian game developers.

Our Mission

To prove that Indian students can build world-class games and sell them globally. We want GamZ to be the launchpad for the next generation of Indian game developers.

Why GamZ?

Every game here is priced affordably in Indian Rupees. You get a trial before you buy. You own the game forever after purchase. No subscriptions. No hidden fees. Just great games at student-friendly prices.
