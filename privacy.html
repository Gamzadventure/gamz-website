Refund Policy

Last updated: April 2025

Our Commitment

We want you to be 100% satisfied with your GamZ purchase. We offer a fair refund policy for digital game purchases.

  • Technical Issues: If the game does not run on your system within 24 hours of purchase, you are eligible for a full refund.
  • Duplicate Purchase: If you accidentally purchased the same game twice, contact us within 48 hours for a full refund of the duplicate.
  • Payment Errors: If you were charged but did not receive download access, you will receive a full refund or immediate access correction.

Non-Eligible Refunds

  • Refund requests made more than 24 hours after purchase (for technical issues)
  • Games that have already been downloaded successfully
  • Requests citing "change of mind" after download

How to Request

Email us at gamzadventure@gmail.com with your Order ID, email, and reason for refund. We will respond within 48 hours.

Processing Time

Approved refunds are processed within 5–7 business days and credited back to your original payment method.

Delivery Policy

Last updated: April 2025

Digital Delivery

GamZ sells digital PC games only. There is no physical shipment involved. All games are delivered digitally via a secure download link immediately after successful payment.

Delivery Process

  • Step 1: Complete your payment on the game's purchase page
  • Step 2: You are automatically redirected to your Download page
  • Step 3: Click "Download Now" to get your game file
  • Step 4: A purchase confirmation is sent to your registered email

Download Access

Your purchased games are permanently accessible from the "My Games" section of your account. You can re-download at any time.

Delivery Failure

If you are not redirected to the download page after payment, or if the download link fails, please contact us immediately at gamzadventure@gmail.com with your Order ID. We will resolve it within 24 hours.
